Our Programs

Our key initiatives are designed to meet the most pressing needs of elders in our community.

Clinic & Home Visits

Providing essential health checkups, medicine, and support to elders, both at our clinic and in their own homes. This includes chronic illness management and mobility support.

Food & Shelter Support

Delivering nutritious food parcels and assisting with critical home repairs to ensure safety and comfort. We focus on ensuring a safe living environment, fixing roofs, and improving sanitation.

Companionship

Fighting loneliness with regular visits from our dedicated volunteers, offering a listening ear and a helping hand. We also organize small community gatherings to foster connection.

Support Our Work

Your gift directly funds these vital programs, providing dignity and care to elders who need it most.
